How It Works

One process. Every filing, license, and contract.

The same four steps carry both practices. Your in-house team should spend its hours on the decisions that move the business, not on assembling submissions or turning standard contracts. Alef handles the assembly. A licensed attorney signs off on every deliverable.

01

Map your obligations

We build the full calendar of every filing, license, and renewal you owe, by jurisdiction and deadline. You see the whole year on one page.

02

Organize your data

We pull your contracts, policies, and records from across your finance systems, ledgers, and records into one structured, filing-ready source.

03

Prepare the submission

We assemble exactly what the regulator expects, formatted, complete, and ready to send. For contracts, redlined and ready to sign.

04

Attorney signs off

A licensed attorney reviews every deliverable before it reaches you or the regulator. Every time.

You keep every playbook, template, and calendar we build. No hiring risk. No retainer. Cancel anytime.
